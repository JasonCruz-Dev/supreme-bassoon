Needs & Wishes

We're a non-profit organization that is dedicated in helping families with their medical needs. Helping less fortunate in our community who find themselves in financial needs and no where to turn.

Our vision is to do everything we can to help those individuals and families who face a temporary financial crisis due to circumstances (such as a hospitalization) and who cannot qualify for most social assistance programs.

Our mission is to help those individuals and families who work hard and give their all and yet have no other alternatives and face the risk of falling into poverty from circumstances beyond their control.
